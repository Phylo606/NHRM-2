About

This app makes it eaiser to perform regular check ups on some patients as it is done remotely. Some people may have find it an anxiety or strain to constantly take to the clinic to do checkups. This app may take care of it by having most of it done remotely.

This app is avaliable to permitted patients with these issues:

  • Pleural effusion

This app also takes care of selected exersises to do with the checkup such as VAS score.
